Our Story

2012

The Small Blog Era

CSNExplore started as "Miles & Memories," a simple blog where our founders shared their backpacking adventures through Southeast Asia. What began as a diary for family and friends quickly resonated with a global audience looking for authentic, unpolished travel advice.

2017

The Platform Evolution

As the community grew, we realized travelers needed more than just stories—they needed tools. We launched our first booking integration and interactive destination maps, officially rebranding as CSNExplore.

Today

A Comprehensive Hub

Today, we support millions of travelers monthly, offering everything from itinerary planning to sustainable travel initiatives. Our mission remains the same: making the world accessible to everyone.
