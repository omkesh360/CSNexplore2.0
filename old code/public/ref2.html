Help Center & FAQs

Quick answers to frequently asked questions.

How do I change my booking?

You can manage your reservations through the 'Bookings' section in your account profile or contact our 24/7 support line.

What is your refund policy?

Refund eligibility depends on the specific destination and provider. Generally, cancellations made 48 hours prior are eligible for partial refunds.

Is travel insurance included?

Standard bookings do not include insurance, but we offer optional comprehensive coverage packages during the checkout process.

Do I need a visa for my trip?

Visa requirements vary by nationality and destination. We provide a 'Visa Info' link on each destination page for your convenience.
