What to expect

Experience the Iron Lady like never before. Meet your guide near the Eiffel Tower and bypass the notoriously long queues to the second floor. As you ascend, listen to fascinating stories about the tower’s construction and Gustave Eiffel’s vision that changed the Paris skyline forever.

From the second floor, you'll enjoy breathtaking 360-degree views of the Louvre, Notre Dame, Montmartre, and the winding Seine River. Your guide will point out landmarks and share hidden gems visible from this vantage point.

Finally, continue your journey to the summit—the highest observation deck in Europe. Toast with a glass of champagne (optional) and see Gustave Eiffel’s restored office. This is the quintessential Paris experience.
